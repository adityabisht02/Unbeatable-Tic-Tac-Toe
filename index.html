Welcome to Unbeatable TicTacToe !

You will be playing against an AI which uses the Alpha Beta pruning search algorithm. The player will never be able to win, he will only be able to draw the game. If you want to skip the tutorial and jump to the game, press "Skip".If you wanna go through the tutorial press "Next".

How it Works

The AI makes the first move in our implementation , hence the min will try to minimise the chances of the player winning. The player will lose if he doesn't make optimal moves on his part . If the player plays optimally he can draw the game, otherwise the minimax algorithm will always manage to win. To optimise the time complexity of the minimax algorithm , we use alpha beta pruning which is basically a modified version of minimax where we prune out branches which we dont need to visit.
